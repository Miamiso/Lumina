<p align="center">
  <strong>A modern, animated, customizable frontend experience with widgets, Discord integration, and achievement support.</strong>
</p>

---

## ✨ Features
◈ Startup Animations: Customizable startup animations sourced from steam deck repo.

◈ Widgets: Useful and interactive widgets directly in the frontend, such as status panels, weather, or custom stickers.

◈ Discord Chat Intergration: Connect your Discord chats and pin friends on the homescreen.

◈ Achievement Support: Support for retro and steam achievements. 

◈ Hyper Customizable: Easily customize themes, colors, layouts, widgets, and music.
